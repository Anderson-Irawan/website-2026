FURARI

Illustrations & Logo

FURARI is making a new arrival based on the local neighbourhood community by recreating a cozy and calm atmosphere whilst having an approach to provide healthier alternatives to typical cat cafe delights. My approach was to create a chill and laidback brand to reflect that feeling.

Furari means to go for a leisurely stroll or for a walk in Japanese. The aim was to let the brand do the talking - from the attitude of its logo, a refreshing palette and in particular, it's lively character.

PROJECT

Illustration + visual identity

DELIVERABLES

Logo • Guidelines
Signage • Collateral

TIMELINE

6-8 Weeks

CATCHING A
TAILWIND

The concept of a cat that really is ready to venture some excitement from a lazy nap is explored in the illustration. The challenge was to channel the casual and chill vibe of the cafe through a character that could resonate with the customers from the get-go. Furari doesn't have to talk your head off. It's got an aura.

There is very much, yeah...fun, but at its foundation, this is a thoughtful design rooted in flexibility. The illustration structure creates a robust brand ecosystem, well-equipped for a range of brand touchpoints all while being built to last and scale. With the brand having no concrete direction at its genesis, the dynamic character has proved to be a flexible playground for a wide range of collateral.

APPETIZING
APPAREL

The colorful art and peculiar, curated food items are rendered as stickers across packaging. The different sticker representations see the brand's hero character adapt to various scenarios, further driving home a versatile visual identity.

It is combined along with strong typographical emphasis to cut through the noise. Furari's palette offers up a cheerful and upbeat sensibility and that is reinforced through the hero typography choices. From the stationery suite to the apparel, it's a brand that has both character and charm.
